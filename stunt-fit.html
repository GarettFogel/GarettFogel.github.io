Stunt Fit

Stunt Fit is a project I started March of 2020. Modern cheerleading is very athletic. The stunting and tumbling in a cheer routine requires speed, strength, and explosiveness. It also requires the conditioning to do those things with perfect technique while dead tired Sport specific training is prevelant in most sports, but not so much in cheerleading. Most cheer workout programs lack structure and direction. Stunt Fit provides periodized strength and conditioning programs designed specifically to enhance cheerleading performance.

I built the Stunt Fit Website myself using PHP, MySQL, JavaScript (jQuery), and CSS. The website contains basic log in log out features as well as a shopping cart and checkout system. Payments are fully integrated into the website using Stripe.

I've also learned some marketing through this. My Instagram page gained 2000+ followers and almost 300,000 impressions in the first few weeks without any paid advertisements. The focus has been on creating as much high quality content as possible in addition to sharing our followers cheerleading videos. This content includes cheerleading tips, nutrition/training information, and snippets of our workout programs. My girlfriend has helped tremendously with this, turning my messages into informative and aesthetically pleasing graphics. Together we have created some viral content that was shared by several cheer accounts with large followings, helping us grow organically very quickily.
