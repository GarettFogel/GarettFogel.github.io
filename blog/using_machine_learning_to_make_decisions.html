Using Machine Learning to Make Decisions: a Simple Text Prediction Script

When you hear the phrase machine learning, your first thought may be complex models with thousands of lines of code or even robots learning how to outsmart humans to take over the world. I'm here to tell you, machine learning isn't neccesarily that complex or scary. At its most basic level, machine learning is the idea that computers can make decisions based on given data rather than being explicitly programmed to make said decisions. Machine learning has applications in nearly every field and is constantly expanding and improving. It can be used by an investor to predict whether the price of a stock is going to go up or down based on previous trends and the historical effects of various outside influences. It can be used by a doctor to predict the likelyhood that a patient has or will develop a disease based on their symptoms and lifestyle compared to the symptoms and lifestyles of others. These machine learning applications allow people to make smart, evidence based decisions.
